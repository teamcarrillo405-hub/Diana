The calm invariant

Attention, never alarm.

Diana is built for real brains and hard weeks. Pressure never helps you start, so the whole system stays calm by design. Three rules hold everywhere.

Amber means attention

When something needs you, it glows a calm amber. No red alarms, no buzzing badges, no panic.

Green means done

Progress shows up quietly. No streaks to break and nothing to lose, so a hard day never sets you back.

Red is never used

Past due work is reframed as something you pick back up, not a failure. The color of alarm has no place here.
