Mitochondria lecture recap

Cleaned transcript 4:12 recording
Mitochondria are the site of cellular respiration. The inner membrane folds into cristae, which increases surface area for the electron transport chain. ATP synthase sits in that membrane and uses the hydrogen gradient to produce ATP. Mrs. Patel said this is the part most people miss on the test — the folding is about surface area, not just storage.
