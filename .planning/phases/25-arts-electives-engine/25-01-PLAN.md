# Phase 25 Plan 01 - Arts & Electives Engine

## Scope

Deliver the v2 arts and electives layer:

- Portfolio builder for student project collections.
- Image/document upload with project metadata and reflection text.
- Art reflection scaffold that asks for student process first.
- Music theory scaffold for major/minor scales, triads, and intervals.
- Drama and speech scaffold for memorization and stage direction annotation.
- AP Art History scaffold using a formal-analysis framework.
- Photography and film scaffold for storyboard and shot-list planning.

## Implementation Notes

- Migration `0026_portfolios_and_electives.sql` adds `portfolios` and `portfolio_items` with owner RLS.
- Portfolio uploads reuse the existing private `note-docs` bucket and render signed image URLs in `/portfolio`.
- `lib/arts/music.ts` keeps note normalization, scales, triads, and interval naming deterministic/testable.
- `lib/arts/scaffold.ts` provides first-run prompts and fallback cards before any AI response.
- `ArtsHelper` appears on arts/electives assignment detail pages and shows prompts before the AI action.
- `arts-scaffold` is a new Edge Function using Claude Haiku 4.5, safety prompts, budget checks, and `ai_interactions` logging.

## Acceptance Mapping

- F110 Portfolio builder: `/portfolio`, `createPortfolio`, `uploadPortfolioFile`, `addPortfolioItem`, `portfolio_items`.
- F111 Art reflection scaffold: `fallbackArtsScaffold("art_reflection")`, `ArtsHelper`.
- F112 Music theory scaffold: `buildScale`, `buildTriad`, `intervalName`, music mode in `ArtsHelper`.
- F113 Drama/Speech scaffold: `drama_speech` mode in local fallback and Edge Function.
- F114 AP Art History: `art_history` mode with formal-analysis prompts/cards.
- F115 Photography/Film: `storyboard` mode with shot-list and sequence cards.
