# Phase 19 Plan 01

## Goal

Implement the Science Scaffold Engine:
- Hypothesis-first prompts before explanation.
- Lab report and scientific-method scaffolds.
- Formula context and chemistry balancing guidance.
- Diagram and FRQ study scaffolds.
- Science assignment surface tied into existing AI traffic-light policy.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Lab assignments and science-like classes show a Science Helper on assignment detail.
- The helper supports hypothesis, lab report, method, formula, chemistry balance, diagram, and FRQ modes.
- The Edge Function enforces red/yellow AI mode restrictions before content-generating help.
- Science scaffolds ask the student to predict, observe, or reason before supplying explanation.
- Formula mode explains variables in the student's context without dumping final answers.
- Chemistry balance mode guides atom counting and coefficient iteration without solving the equation outright.
- Diagram mode produces Mermaid source that the student can inspect or reuse.
- `science-scaffold` is deployed and ACTIVE in Supabase.
- Static gates remain green: typecheck, Vitest, tone-audit, diff check, production build.
