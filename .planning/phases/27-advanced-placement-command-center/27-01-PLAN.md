# Phase 27 Plan 01 - Advanced Placement Command Center

## Scope

Deliver the v2 AP preparation layer:

- AP exam countdown with calm milestones.
- FRQ scaffold per AP format.
- MCQ practice explanations for every answer choice.
- Score-band predictor with shame-free language.
- AP subject-specific study plan generator.
- College Board / AP format alignment across supported subjects.

## Implementation Notes

- Migration `0028_ap_command_center.sql` adds `ap_exam_plans` and `ap_practice_attempts` with owner RLS.
- `/ap` is the command center for exam plans, FRQ format reminders, practice attempts, and score bands.
- `lib/ap/command.ts` keeps AP subject coverage, format mapping, score bands, FRQ outlines, MCQ parsing, and milestone plans deterministic/testable.
- `ApHelper` appears on AP/test-prep assignment detail pages and wraps the new `ap-scaffold` Edge Function.
- `ap-scaffold` uses Claude Haiku 4.5 with safety prompts, budget checks, and `ai_interactions` logging.

## Acceptance Mapping

- F121 AP exam countdown: `/ap`, `daysUntilExam`, `apMilestonePlan`.
- F122 FRQ scaffold: `fallbackApScaffold`, `ApHelper`, `ap-scaffold`.
- F123 MCQ practice: `ApMcqQuestion`, `normalizeQuestions`, `ApHelper`.
- F124 Score predictor: `scoreBand`, `/ap` practice result form.
- F125 Study plan generator: `apMilestonePlan`, `/ap` active plan card.
- F126 Format alignment: AP subject-to-format mapping in `AP_SUBJECTS`.
